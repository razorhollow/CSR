Services We Offer

Depositions

Numerous years of experience and countless depositions, ranging from videotaped expert testimony to simple subject matter, qualify us to provide you with same-day rough drafts and expedited delivery of your transcripts in various formats depending on your litigation needs.

Realtime

Realtime is an extremely valuable tool that provides the client with immediate transcription of the reporter’s notes during depositions onsite or remotely through various platforms.

Arbitrations

Whether going to trial or not, having a verbatim record of your next arbitration can help to avoid and/or remedy any issues that may arise.

Public Hearings

The subject matter of public hearings can oftentimes be related to controversial topics. However, having a court reporter present at the hearing to take down public comments guarantees delivery of an accurate transcription of events.

Grand Jury

With over 10 years of grand jury reporting experience, we understand the importance grand jury plays in our criminal justice system and the high level of confidentiality that is critical during the proceedings.

Trials

As the guardian of the record, arranging for a court reporter at your next trial ensures a verbatim transcript for easy readbacks to the jury and/or subsequent appeals.

Workers' Compensation Deposition

The opinions of the treating doctors and IMEs are critical in Workers’ Compensation cases. We know how important it is to have a highly-trained court reporter, with vast medical terminology knowledge, present to take down the testimony regarding examinations, tests and level of disability.

Audio Recordings

In times a court reporter was not present during a civil case or you simply have a recording that you need transcribed, our transcription service enables you to receive a verbatim transcript of the audio.
