Christine is a 2007 graduate of Alfred State College in the field of Court and Realtime Reporting.  In 2015, she obtained her bachelor’s in Technology Management from Alfred State College.

A member of the NCRA, Christine is well versed in multiple areas of the court reporting field, including depositions, trials, Workers’ Compensation cases, public hearings, expert witness testimony and anything else you need a written record for.  She continually strives to keep up on the latest technological advancements and is dedicated to implementing them into her services.

Christine has tremendous drive and motivation to produce a quality, timely, work product all parties are impressed with. With nine years of professional experience, her love for the job is evident in the workplace.
